True spirit of the West About Crazy Bob's

Crazy Bob's started as a crazy idea of Robert Swannson. He wanted to combine western theatre and fine dining, a crazy idea or so he thought. After pitching the idea to entrepreneurs, David S. Baum and Phineas F. Cord. Crazy Bob's wasn't such a crazy idea after all. Crazy Bob's have provided exciting shows about the life of the wild west while serving quality food for three decades. Come join us for the experience of a life time.
